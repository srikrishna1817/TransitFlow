# TransitFlow Advanced Scheduling Features

## Overview
Phase 2B introduces intelligent scheduling capabilities tailored for Hyderabad Metro Rail (HMRL) operations.

## Features

### 1. Route Optimization Engine
Intelligently assigns trains to HMRL's 3 operational routes based on:
- Train health scores
- Route requirements
- Depot locations
- Historical performance

**HMRL Routes:**
- **Red Line**: Miyapur ↔ LB Nagar (29.87 km, 27 stations, 25 trains required)
- **Blue Line**: Nagole ↔ Raidurg (28 km, 23 stations, 23 trains required)
- **Green Line**: JBS Parade Ground ↔ MGBS (9.6 km, 9 stations, 12 trains required)

### 2. Crew Scheduling System
Manages driver and conductor assignments with full compliance:
- Indian labor law compliance (8-hour shifts, 12-hour rest, 48-hour weekly limit)
- Route certification management
- Multi-language crew assignment (Telugu, Hindi, English)
- Depot-based crew allocation

### 3. What-If Scenario Analyzer
Simulates real-world disruptions:
- Train breakdowns on critical routes
- Ameerpet interchange disruptions
- Monsoon impact on operations
- Tech hub rush (Blue Line surge)
- Depot maintenance days

### 4. Multi-Day Rolling Planner
Plans operations over weeks/months:
- Weekly schedules with maintenance windows
- Monthly rotation for mileage balancing
- Festival calendar integration
- Certificate renewal planning
